About Us

If you're looking for delicious, healthy meat options that won't break the bank, then you'll want to check out our restaurant! We offer a variety of meat options, all of which are sourced from local farms. We also have a variety of side dishes that go great with our entrees, including salads and pasta dishes. Our menu also features several vegetarian and vegan options that will suit all tastes and preferences. Our goal is to offer a convenient and affordable meal option to our customers without compromising on taste or quality. Come to visit us at your earliest convenience and see for yourself!

Our menu features a number of protein-based entrees that are sure to satisfy all of our customers' needs. We have a number of steak options on our menu, including sirloin steak and ribeye steak. Our steaks will be sourced directly from trusted farms and high-quality ingredients will cut down on prices. Sides served at every meal will include salads, soups, and of course fries or vegetables. We also have burgers and other non-meat items for those looking for something a little different. All of our meals are cooked to perfection and served with a side of fries or a side of salad. We offer a wide selection of sides and desserts as well, so there's something for everyone at our shop!

Whether it's happy hour after work or a late night out with friends, nothing says relaxation and celebration like time spent on an outdoor yard with a drink in your hand. Next time you feel closed in by the walls around you, step outside to enjoy one of the best outdoor resto-bars and grills in Philippines. MeatHub focuses on “Fresh, Quality Pinoy-Putok-batok Food” with an emphasis on small shareable plates that go well with beer.

We are located in Ayala Avenue 1209 Makati City Metro Manila and are open from 8 am to 10 pm every day. The atmosphere at our restaurants is casual and relaxed, so the whole family is welcome!

Steak

A steak is a thick cut of meat generally sliced across the muscle fibers, sometimes including a bone. It is normally grilled or fried. Steak can be diced, cooked in sauce, such as in steak and kidney pie, or minced and formed into patties, such as hamburgers.

Ribeye Steak

Ribeyes are loaded with marbling giving them a whole lot of flavor. This is why many consider these to be the tastiest of all steaks.

Tenderloin Steak

The tenderloin steak is a cut from the loin of a cow. Also known as a filet mignon steak.

Strip Steak

You may have tried a strip steak without even knowing it. This strip comes from the short loin area of a cow.

Hamburger

A hamburger, or simply burger, is a food consisting of fillings—usually a patty of ground meat, typically beef—placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll. Hamburgers are often served with cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, bacon, or chilis; condiments such as ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, relish, or a "special sauce", often a variation of Thousand Island dressing; and are frequently placed on sesame seed buns. A hamburger patty topped with cheese is called a cheeseburger.

Broiled Beef Burger

Distinctive taste and irresistible flavour in every bite.

Veggie Burger

Veggie burgers are not easy to make awesome, but we think this one nails it.

Mushroom Burger

Ready to turn over a new burger? I guarantee no one will be missing the beef after tasting these vegetarian burgers.

Pizza

There is absolutely no denying that pizza is one of the best foods ever invented. Italian food in general is top tier, but pizza holds the gauntlet as it is, perhaps, the best food ever created. The best thing about pizza is that it is totally customizable. From the base, to the sauce, to the cheese, to the toppings, you can choose different options to create a pizza that is totally unique to you. If you have ever sat and thought about pizza, like lots of us have, you might be wondering which toppings are the most popular.

Pepperoni

For so many Filipinos, pepperoni is the ultimate pizza topping, and this is primarily because it is a classic.

Sausage

Some people prefer mild or sweet sausages, others prefer sausages with a real meaty flavor, and some people like spicy sausage.

Mushrooms

Even if you don't like mushrooms in other meals, it is definitely worth trying them as a topping on your pizza.
