About Us

If you're looking for delicious, healthy meat options that won't break the bank, then you'll want to check out our restaurant! We offer a variety of meat options, all of which are sourced from local farms. We also have a variety of side dishes that go great with our entrees, including salads and pasta dishes. Our menu also features several vegetarian and vegan options that will suit all tastes and preferences. Our goal is to offer a convenient and affordable meal option to our customers without compromising on taste or quality. Come to visit us at your earliest convenience and see for yourself!

Our menu features a number of protein-based entrees that are sure to satisfy all of our customers' needs. We have a number of steak options on our menu, including sirloin steak and ribeye steak. Our steaks will be sourced directly from trusted farms and high-quality ingredients will cut down on prices. Sides served at every meal will include salads, soups, and of course fries or vegetables. We also have burgers and other non-meat items for those looking for something a little different. All of our meals are cooked to perfection and served with a side of fries or a side of salad. We offer a wide selection of sides and desserts as well, so there's something for everyone at our shop!

Whether it’s happy hour after work or a late night out with friends, nothing says relaxation and celebration like time spent on an outdoor yard with a drink in your hand. Next time you feel closed in by the walls around you, step outside to enjoy one of the best outdoor resto-bars and grills in Philippines. MeatHub focuses on “Fresh, Quality Pinoy-Putok-batok Food” with an emphasis on small shareable plates that go well with beer.

We are located in Ayala Avenue 1209 Makati City Metro Manila and are open from 8 am to 10 pm every day. The atmosphere at our restaurants is casual and relaxed, so the whole family is welcome!
