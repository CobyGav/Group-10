Pizza

There is absolutely no denying that pizza is one of the best foods ever invented. Italian food in general is top tier, but pizza holds the gauntlet as it is, perhaps, the best food ever created. The best thing about pizza is that it is totally customizable. From the base, to the sauce, to the cheese, to the toppings, you can choose different options to create a pizza that is totally unique to you. If you have ever sat and thought about pizza, like lots of us have, you might be wondering which toppings are the most popular.

Pepperoni

For so many Filipinos, pepperoni is the ultimate pizza topping, and this is primarily because it is a classic.

Sausage

Some people prefer mild or sweet sausages, others prefer sausages with a real meaty flavor, and some people like spicy sausage.

Mushrooms

Even if you don't like mushrooms in other meals, it is definitely worth trying them as a topping on your pizza.

Bacon

Even though bacon is usually associated with breakfast dishes, there is no dish which it can't make better.

Onions

Onions are a versatile topping for most food and pizza is no exception.

Cheese Overload

For some, this might not count as a topping, but for us, there isn't much better than extra cheese on a pizza.

Peppers

Peppers are extremely versatile vegetables, and any color will taste excellent when cooked on a pizza base.

Chicken

Chicken isn't a traditional items for topping your pizza, but it is still one that a lot of people opt for.

Black Olives

This is another potentially controversial topping, but black olives can give your pizza a salty kick.

Spinach

If you are a lover of vegetables, then you probably really enjoy a spinach topping on your pizza.

Tomato and Basil

If you are going for a classic style margarita pizza, then the perfect topping is tomato and basil.

Beef

If you are a fan of a meat lovers pizza, then beef is probably a topping that you are very familiar with.

Ham

If you aren't a diehard fan of the controversial ham and pineapple pizza, you might prefer just ham as your topping of choice.

Pesto

Tomato-based sauces on pizza is a hill that some people are willing to die on, a pizza that uses pesto as its base sauce is far too much.

Pulled Pork

If you are a fan of takeaway pizza, then you have probably tried pulled pork as a topping.

Ham And Pineapple

When we spoke of controversial pizza toppings, you already knew that this was the one that we were speaking about.
